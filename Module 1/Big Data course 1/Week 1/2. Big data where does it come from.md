# Major sources of big data -
  1. Machine
       - data generated from real time sensors in industrial machinery or vehicles that logs that track user behavior online, environmental sensors or personal health trackers, and many other sense data resources.
  2. People
       - we refer to the vast amount of social media data, status updates, tweets, photos, and medias.
  3. Organization
      - Traditional data
        -  including transaction information in databases and structured data open stored in data warehouses.
**Big data can be structured, unstructured, or semi structured**
***Real value of big data comes from combining these sources, the sources as individuals
are not of much use. This integrated data is further used for further integration and
becomes a source for big data***

- After having the data sources, it then enables us to have _** data enabled decisions and actions**_
